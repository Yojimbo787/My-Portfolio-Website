Welcome to my portfolio page. Here are some of the websites that I have worked on or done as projects to get used to building websites from scratch. I also describe what process I did and also the technologies that I used in those projects so that you can understand what I do. Please take the time to have a look at them be much appreciated.

Project One

In this project I built I was aiming to build a page that was in the theme of a bespoke tour guide company who offered services across Japan to take customers on a tour around various cites in Japan. I used a combination of Bootstrap 4 with some CSS that I put in there for custom styling. I also used Font Awesome Icons for some of the items on the page such as the check boxes. Was a project I really did enjoy doing and made me fall in love with using Bootstrap for building webpages in a very quick and efficient way
